About me

Born in Yorkshire, I graduated from Reigate School of Art with a BA (Hons). Having grown up passionate about the outdoors and wildlife these form the subjects of most of my paintings. I start out using watercolour but have later produced artwork using oils and pastel and more recently in mixed media.

My work has been exhibited widely at galleries in London and regionally across the UK as well as in France, Germany and America. My paintings have been published as greetings cards, fine art prints and I have illustrated a number of books and magazines.

I am an associate member of the Guild of Railway Artists . Formed in 1970, it allows artist like myself to depict and publicly present railway scenes of the past and present. One of my largest commissions to date was a large permanent wall mural for the reception area of Ashford International railway station that encompassed a countryside theme of the local area.

As well as painting English landscapes and nature, I have travelled through and painted sceneries of France. Hope you enjoy my artwork as much as I enoyed painting them. Please get in contact if you would like to leave a comment or to enquire about purchasing one of my paintings.
